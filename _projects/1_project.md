I am interested in both theory and application of bounded-confidence models on graphs. I use these models to study the effects of ideology and quality in content spread online, and how media can influence these dynamics. I also use mean-field approximations to study the existence and dynamics of solutions, including bifurcations and basins of attraction of the steady states. My collaborators and I are developing generalizations of bounded-confidence models for hypergraphs, multilayer networks, and simplicial complexes.
